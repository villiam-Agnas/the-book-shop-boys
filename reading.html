Book i am currently reading

Fahrenheit 451

Fahrenheit 451 is a dystopian novel set in a future where books are banned and firemen burn them to suppress independent thought. The story follows Guy Montag, a fireman who begins to question his role and the empty, controlled world around him. Encounters with people who value curiosity and knowledge awaken his doubts, pushing him toward a dangerous search for meaning. The novel explores themes of censorship, conformity, and the power of ideas, showing how a society built on distraction and fear loses its humanity. It is both a gripping tale and a timeless warning about the cost of silence.
